About Us

Banali | loveliest village.

Banali is a Village in Dwarikhal Block in Pauri Garhwal District of Uttarakhand State, India. It is located 70 KM towards South from District head quarters Pauri. 135 KM from State capital Dehradun

Banali is surrounded by Kaljikhal Block towards North , Nainidanda Block towards East , Rikhnikhal Block towards North , Sult Block towards East

Banali Hills | An Unforgetten Place

Spread over more than 10 acres of forest land and still an unexplored hilltop village, an altitude of 1700 M/5700 ft. surrounded by dense forest with amazing 360-degree valley view of Shivalik range (Lansdowne, Jaiharikhal, Gumkhal, Ketukhal etc) and panoramic view of Himalayas (In local language - Dudh ki toli).

The pure air and sweet spring water, serene environ amidst verdant surroundings rejuvenate the mind, the body and the soul. Our Mud house have been painstakingly built in the local stone
